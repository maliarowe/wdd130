Georgia

Georgia is a state located in the southeastern region of the United States. It is bordered to the north by Tennessee and North Carolina, to the northeast by South Carolina, to the southeast by the Atlantic Ocean, to the south by Florida, and to the west by Alabama. Georgia is known for its diverse terrain, which includes beaches, farmland, and mountains. The state capital and largest city is Atlanta, a major economic and cultural hub.
