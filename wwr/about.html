History

Founded in the roaring rapids of 1995, White Water Rafting Company emerged from the passion of a group of avid adventurers who shared a love for the great outdoors and the rush of white water. What began as a small team of enthusiasts guiding friends down local rivers quickly evolved into a renowned adventure company, offering unforgettable rafting experiences to thrill-seekers from around the globe. With safety, excitement, and environmental stewardship at our core, we've grown to become a trusted name in the industry, providing expert-guided trips and creating lasting memories for adventurers of all ages. Join us on our journey as we continue to navigate the wild waters and share the joy of rafting with the world!
